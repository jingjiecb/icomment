Embed Code

Add this code to your blog posts where you want comments to appear:

<div id="icomment"></div> <script src="http://your-domain.com:7001/static/comment.js" data-api="http://your-domain.com:7001"></script>

Replace your-domain.com:7001 with your iComment server's domain and port.
